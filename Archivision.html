Project Overview

Archivision is a digital platform designed to streamline the management and presentation of architectural portfolios. The project aimed to create a user-friendly interface for architects and designers to showcase their work, organize project assets, and collaborate with clients or teams efficiently.

Archivision is especially intended for architecture students to create and present their portfolios in a professional and organized manner.

The primary challenge was to balance a visually compelling design with robust functionality, ensuring that users could easily upload, categorize, and share high-resolution images, drawings, and project documentation.

Design Process

01

User Research & Personas

Conducted interviews and surveys with architects and designers to understand their workflow, pain points, and portfolio needs.

02

Information Architecture

Mapped out content structures and navigation flows to ensure intuitive access to projects, assets, and collaboration tools.

03

Wireframing & Prototyping

Developed low- and high-fidelity wireframes, iterating on layouts and interactions based on user feedback and usability testing.

04

Visual Design & Branding

Created a modern, minimal visual identity with a focus on clarity, whitespace, and showcasing project imagery.

05

Development Collaboration

Worked closely with developers to ensure design fidelity, responsive layouts, and smooth asset management features.

Results & Impact

The implementation of the Archivision brand identity and platform design resulted in a seamless and engaging user experience for architecture students and professionals alike. By grounding the design process in user research and iterative prototyping, the final product addressed real workflow challenges‚Äîmaking it easier to organize, present, and share portfolios. The intuitive information architecture and clean visual language empowered users to showcase their work with confidence, while the collaborative features fostered stronger connections between students, educators, and clients. As a result, Archivision not only elevated the quality of portfolio presentations but also contributed to increased user satisfaction, higher engagement rates, and positive feedback from the architectural community.
