StayEase - Hotel Management

Complete visual identity redesign for a tech startup, including logo, guidelines, and marketing materials

Branding Design PHP, CSS, JS Strategy Adobe Creative Suite

Project Overview

This project presents a comprehensive brand identity system for a modern hotel management company. The goal was to create a visual identity that reflects the company's commitment to exceptional guest experiences, operational excellence, and hospitality innovation. The system is designed to be flexible for use across a portfolio of hotels, from luxury resorts to boutique properties.

The challenge was to develop a cohesive brand language that appeals to both business and leisure travelers, supports digital and print applications, and elevates the perception of the hotel group in a competitive market.
