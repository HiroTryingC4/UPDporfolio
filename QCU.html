QCU Portal - University System

Complete visual identity redesign for a tech startup, including logo, guidelines, and marketing materials

Branding Design PHP, CSS, JS Strategy Adobe Creative Suite

Project Overview

The QCU Portal for Students is a web-based platform designed to streamline academic and administrative processes for students of Quezon City University. The portal provides a centralized hub where students can access grades, class schedules, announcements, and essential university services.

The main objective was to enhance the student experience by offering a user-friendly interface, real-time updates, and secure access to personal academic information. The portal supports efficient communication between students and university departments, reducing manual paperwork and improving overall engagement.

Brand Elements

Logo Design

Developed a timeless logo that evokes comfort, elegance, and hospitality, adaptable for both luxury and boutique hotel brands.

Color Palette

Selected a palette of warm neutrals and sophisticated accent colors to convey relaxation, trust, and modernity.

Typography

Chose a clean, approachable typeface pairing for clarity and a welcoming tone across all guest and corporate communications.

Visual Language

Established a visual system using inviting imagery, subtle patterns, and generous whitespace to reflect the guest-centric philosophy.

Results & Impact

The QCU Portal for Students delivered a transformative experience for the university community. By grounding the design process in real user needs and iterative feedback, the portal achieved high adoption rates among students and staff. The intuitive interface and clear visual identity fostered trust and engagement, while streamlined workflows reduced administrative workload and improved communication. As a result, the university saw increased student satisfaction, faster access to academic information, and a stronger sense of connection within the campus community.
