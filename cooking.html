Dishcraft Web App

A modern web application for discovering, sharing, and organizing authentic Filipino recipes

Web App UI/UX Filipino Cuisine React Community

Project Overview

Dishcraft is a web application designed to celebrate and preserve Filipino culinary heritage. The platform enables users to explore a curated collection of traditional and modern Filipino recipes, contribute their own dishes, and connect with a community passionate about Filipino food. The goal was to create an intuitive, visually appealing, and mobile-friendly experience that makes it easy for anyone to discover, cook, and share Filipino recipes.

The project focused on building a user-friendly interface, robust search and filtering features, and community-driven content to foster engagement and cultural appreciation among users both in the Philippines and abroad.

Design Process

01

User Research & Persona Development

Conducted interviews and surveys with home cooks, food bloggers, and culinary enthusiasts to understand their needs, motivations, and challenges in finding and sharing Filipino recipes online.

02

Information Architecture & Wireframing

Mapped out user flows and created wireframes to ensure easy navigation, clear categorization of recipes, and seamless contribution of new dishes.

03

Visual Design & Branding

Developed a vibrant visual identity inspired by Filipino culture, incorporating traditional patterns, warm colors, and appetizing food imagery to create an inviting atmosphere.

04

Prototyping & Usability Testing

Built interactive prototypes and conducted usability tests with target users to refine the interface, improve recipe submission flows, and optimize mobile responsiveness.

05

Development & Launch

Implemented the web app using React and modern web technologies, integrated community features, and launched Dishcraft to the public with ongoing feedback and iteration.

Brand Elements

Logo Design

Developed a timeless logo that evokes comfort, elegance, and hospitality, adaptable for both luxury and boutique hotel brands.

Color Palette

Selected a palette of warm neutrals and sophisticated accent colors to convey relaxation, trust, and modernity.

Typography

Chose a clean, approachable typeface pairing for clarity and a welcoming tone across all guest and corporate communications.

Visual Language

Established a visual system using inviting imagery, subtle patterns, and generous whitespace to reflect the guest-centric philosophy.

Results & Impact

The Dishcraft web app delivered a seamless and engaging experience that directly reflected the insights gathered during user research and the thoughtful design process. By prioritizing intuitive navigation, visually rich branding, and robust community features, the platform empowered users to easily discover, contribute, and organize Filipino recipes. This user-centered approach resulted in rapid community growth, with a notable increase in recipe submissions and active participation from Filipino food enthusiasts worldwide.
